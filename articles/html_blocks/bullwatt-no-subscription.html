BullWatt allows you to train indoors without the need for a subscription. By leveraging web technologies and Bluetooth FTMS, you can connect directly to your smart trainer and perform structured workouts without any recurring fees. This guide will walk you through the steps to get started and make the most of your indoor cycling sessions.

What you don't have to worry about

  • No Subscription Fees: BullWatt is completely free to use, allowing you to access all features without any recurring costs.
  • No Software Downloads: Since BullWatt runs in your web browser, there's no need to download or install any software on your device.
  • No Complex Setup: Connecting to your smart trainer is straightforward with Bluetooth FTMS, and the app guides you through the process.

Quick guide : Getting Started

  • Access BullWatt: Open Google Chrome and navigate to the BullWatt web app.
  • Select a Workout: Browse through the catalog of structured workouts available on BullWatt. Choose one that fits your training goals and FTP level.
  • Connect Your Trainer: Use Bluetooth to connect your smart trainer to the app. Ensure your trainer supports the FTMS protocol for ERG mode functionality.
  • Start Training: Follow the on-screen instructions to begin your workout. The app will control your trainer's resistance according to the workout profile, allowing you to focus on your performance.
  • Track Your Progress: After completing your session, you can export your activity data or sync it with Strava to keep a record of your training history.
