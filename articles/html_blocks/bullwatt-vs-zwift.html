Some people pay to ride indoor

Many cyclists choose platforms like Zwift, which require a subscription fee, to access a wide range of virtual routes, structured workouts, and social features. These services offer a polished experience with extensive community engagement and gamification elements.

Others prefer to use open-source or free alternatives like BullWatt, which allow them to train without recurring costs. These platforms focus on core training functionalities, privacy, and community-driven development, providing a minimalist yet effective indoor cycling experience.
