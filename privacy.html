Data privacy

Effective Date: 2025-02-10

Bullwatt is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal data. This Data Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you visit or use our website, www.bullwatt.com (the "Website").

1. Information We Collect:

  We may collect the following types of personal data from you:
  
  Information you provide directly: This includes information you provide when you contact us (e.g., name, email address, phone number).
  Information we collect automatically: When you visit the Website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring website, pages visited, devices used, and timestamps. 
  We may use cookies to collect this information.

2. How We Use Your Information:

  We may use your personal data for the following purposes:
  
  Providing and improving the Website: We use your data to operate, maintain, and improve the Website, and to personalize your experience.
  Responding to your inquiries: We use your data to respond to your requests and questions.
  Legal compliance: We may use your data to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. How We Share Your Information:

  We may share your personal data with the following categories of third parties:
  
  Service providers: We may share your data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer service. 
  We ensure these providers are contractually obligated to protect your data. 
  Legal authorities: We may disclose your data to legal authorities if required by law or legal process.
  Third parties : We may share your data with Strava if you activate this service.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

  We use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your activity on the Website.  
  You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Website.

5. Your Rights:

  You may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as:
  
  Access: You may have the right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  Correction: You may have the right to correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  Deletion: You may have the right to request that we delete your personal data.
  Objection: You may have the right to object to the processing of your personal data.
  Data portability: You may have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  To exercise these rights, please contact us at charles@superbarbicane.com.

6. Data Security:

  We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy:

  Our Website is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under this age.

8. Changes to this Privacy Notice:

  We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time.  We will post the updated Privacy Notice on the Website, and the revised Notice will be effective upon posting.

9. Contact Us:

  If you have any questions about this Privacy Notice, please contact us at:
  
  charles@superbarbicane.com
